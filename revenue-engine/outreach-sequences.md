# Outreach Sequences

> All outreach is bylined as **"The Team"** — no personal names appear in any template.
> Personalize bracketed fields `[like this]` before sending.

---

## Sequence 1 — LinkedIn Cold Outreach: IP Licensing

**Audience:** CTOs, VPs Engineering, Heads of Platform at Series B–D SaaS companies.
**Goal:** Qualify commercial licensing fit and book a 20-minute discovery call.

Use a 5-touch nurture sequence. Wait 5–7 business days between each message unless they respond.

---

### Message 1 — Connection Request Note (300 chars max)

> Hi [First Name], we're the team behind the Squad multi-agent orchestration framework (open source, MIT). Saw [Company] is scaling its AI stack — thought there might be relevant overlap. Happy to connect.
>
> — The Team @ Ventures IP

---

### Message 2 — First Follow-up (sent after connection accepted, day 1–2)

> Thanks for connecting, [First Name].
>
> Quick context: Squad is a multi-agent AI orchestration framework we publish under MIT for individual/OSS use, with commercial licensing for companies embedding it in products at scale.
>
> Companies using AI agents in production — especially at the platform or infrastructure layer — often reach a point where they need SLA-backed support, white-label rights, or legal indemnification. That's the commercial tier.
>
> Is any of that relevant to what [Company] is building? Happy to send over the framework overview if so.
>
> — The Team

---

### Message 3 — Value Add (day 7–9 after message 2, no response)

> [First Name] — sharing this in case it's useful regardless of timing.
>
> We published a case study on how Squad's agent routing pattern cut deployment overhead by ~60% for an engineering team. [Link to case study or article]
>
> No ask — just thought it might be relevant to what you're working on.
>
> — The Team

---

### Message 4 — Soft CTA (day 14–16 after message 3, no response)

> One more note, [First Name] — then I'll leave you alone.
>
> We're working with a handful of companies this quarter on commercial licensing arrangements. Pricing starts at $4,800/yr for the Commercial tier — covers white-labeling, SLA support, and legal indemnification for production deployments.
>
> If there's any interest in a 20-minute call to see if it fits, I'm happy to find time. Otherwise, no pressure — the framework stays MIT and free.
>
> — The Team

---

### Message 5 — Breakup Message (day 21–25, no response)

> [First Name] — closing the loop on my end.
>
> If the timing is off or Squad isn't a fit, totally understood. I'll stop reaching out.
>
> If the situation changes — especially if you're scaling AI agent infrastructure or commercializing a product that uses orchestration patterns — feel free to reach back out.
>
> Best,
> The Team @ Ventures IP

## Sequence 2 — Email: Consulting Inquiry Response

**Trigger:** Someone submits the site contact form or emails with a consulting question.
**Goal:** Qualify the opportunity and schedule a discovery call within 48 hours.

---

**Subject:** Re: Your inquiry — next steps

> Hi [First Name],
>
> Thanks for reaching out. We appreciate you taking the time.
>
> A few quick questions to make sure we scope this correctly before getting on a call:
>
> 1. What's the rough timeline you're working against?
> 2. Is this greenfield (new system) or improving an existing implementation?
> 3. What does the current state look like — team size, tech stack, where you're stuck?
>
> Based on what you've described, this sounds like it could be a good fit for our [Architecture Review / AI Agent Design / Strategic Advisory] track. Our rate for that work starts at [$X/hr], and most engagements in this category run [4–20 hours].
>
> If that's in the right ballpark, I can send a Calendly link to find 30 minutes this week.
>
> — The Team
> Ventures IP
> [site URL]

## Quick Reference — Messaging Pillars

Use these proof points in any outreach to reinforce credibility:

| Pillar | Talking Point |
|--------|--------------|
| **Open source trust** | MIT-licensed, publicly auditable, community-adopted |
| **Production-proven** | Deployed in real engineering teams, not just demos |
| **Flexible commercials** | Starts at $4,800/yr; enterprise custom; consulting a la carte |
| **Fast to value** | Template Pack → live Squad setup in under a day |
| **No lock-in** | Commercial license adds rights; doesn't restrict MIT core |
